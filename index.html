My Autobiography

Hello there,

My name is Keviweto Yhoshu and i am from Nagaland in India.I am 11 years old.I study in Livingstone Foudation International(L.F.I.)


So,let's talk about where i live.

I live in kacharigaon, a village in Dimapur.It is a very nice area in the villages where it is quite exept when cars are around.The houses over here in Kacharigaon is a bit mixed up, in another way, there are many different structures of houses from RCC buildings to wooden kacha houses.There are four khels/blocks in kacharigaon.(as much as I know!) They are,Khel 'A',Khel 'B',Khel 'C' and Khel'D'.


Now,let's talk about my family.

My small family consists of 6 members, my father,my mother,my siblings and myself. Funfact!

My grandfather said that he and his parents hid in the jungle during world war 2 and survived without getting hit by a bullet. They had to run to the jungle because the war hit that particular area which they were living at,at that time. Now in the plot of land of my Great grandfather Vicha, we built a small hotel where guest rented rooms during hornbill festival.Different types of people came to this hotel. As i correctly remember there were different indians from different states,Americans, some Brittish,French,Japanese,people from the Netherlands and people from Isreal.
